Sushi

Description

Sushi is a delicious dish made from white rice and raw fish. It originates from Asian cultures and is enjoyed by humans everywhere around the world. There are several varieties, but the one we are focusing on is a Salmon Roll.

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Avocado
  • Rice
  • Seaweed Paper

Steps

  1. Cook the rice
  2. Dice the salmon
  3. Dice the avocado
  4. Place seaweed paper onto bamboo roller
  5. Add a layer the rice onto bamboo roller
  6. Add the salmon and avocado
  7. Roll the bamboo
  8. Remove from bamboo and enjoy!
