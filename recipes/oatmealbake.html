Oatmeal Bake

Description

Oatmeal bakes are the perfect way to satisfy a sweettooth in a nutritious way. These take minimal effort and give you enough fibre for one day!

Ingredients

  • Oatmeal
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Bananas
  • 1 whole egg
  • vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Grind your oatmeal in a blender or food processor
  2. Beat your egg with your liquid ingredients
  3. Add the oatmeal flour from the blender
  4. Springle chocolate chips throughout the batter and on top
  5. Bake in the oven at 200 degrees celsius for 10 minutes
