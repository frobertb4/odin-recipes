Superfood Flex Smoothie

Description

The Superfood flex is a Barry's favourite among the fuel Bar. This smoothie filled with acai, blueberries, and banana will keep you satiated and recovered after a sweaty workout.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cup Coconut Water
  • 1 bar of frozen acai
  • 1/2 cup blueberries
  • 1/2 cup bananas
  • 1 scoop vanilla whey protein
  • 1 table spoon almond butter
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon maca powder
  • 1 teaspoon flaxseeds
  • 1 teaspoon glutamine
  • 1 cup ice

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients into a blender
  2. Blend on a high speed for 30-45 seconds
