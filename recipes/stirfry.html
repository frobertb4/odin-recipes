Chicken Stir Fry

Description

A chicken stir fry is a quick and easy meal pack with proteins, vitamins, and nutrients.

Ingredients

  • 500 g raw chicken
  • 1 cup rice
  • A bunch of veggies
  • Stir fry sauce

Directions

  1. Cook your chicken
  2. Cook your rice
  3. Stir fry the vegetables in the sauce
  4. Add chicken to the stir fry
  5. Serve warm
