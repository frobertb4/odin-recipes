BBQ Chicken Pizza

Description

Pizza is a classic italian dish, but American have put their spin on it with some BBQ chicken. You get to enjoy the deliciousness of bread and sauce, but the sauce has been substituted by barbecue sauce instead of marinara or napoli sauce according to the italian tradition. Instead of meat from a pork variety, this is seasoned, barbecue chicken.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Chicken
  • Barbecue saice
  • Onions
  • Jalapenos

Steps

  1. Season and cook the chicken
  2. Chop the onions and peppers
  3. Flatten the pizza dough
  4. Add the BBQ Sauce onto the dough
  5. Add all the toppings
  6. Bake in the oven for 15 minutes at 450 degrees
  7. Let it cool for 2 minutes and enjoy!
